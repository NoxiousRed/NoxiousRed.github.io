Dine On Campus

The Promise:

Are you a student on UNCG campus? Are you looking for some good food to get you through a tough study session? If so, then look no further. The promise of Dine On Campus is to provide you all that you need to do well without needing to worry about culinary calculations. Whether you're in the mood for a dense meal or a light snack, Dine On Campus will keep you on the right track!
